Features
You can use the Clues list to test if the word exists. Using the Add button will only clear the word if it exists in the puzzle.

Once the Clues list is loaded you can even use it offline.

The Clues list will remember all of your entries if you need to reload you browser or reboot your computer. If you want to move computers - use the Bulk Grab and Add features at the bottom.

The Clues page is normally updated just after flip on most days and if not - normally within a few hours.

Total keyboard less entry - You can use the mouse on the Grid letters, and even erase the last letter with the <-.

Find 2 letter words in the list easily : Just type in the two letters in the Add Word box and hit the ? button - It will scroll you down the list to that point.

If in a new Grid you don't seem to have the latest list - or your old words are still there - Try and refresh your page with Cntrl F5 or hold the Shift key down when you click on the 'Reload' Button. This works in Firefox as well as IE.
